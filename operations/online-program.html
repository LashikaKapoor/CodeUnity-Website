Online Program

What we offer

  • Custom Learning Notes — curated by experienced student mentors covering languages like Python and fundamentals.
  • Weekly Online Sessions — live Q&A, project check-ins, and guided lessons.
  • Real Coding Projects — hands-on tasks with feedback; submit work to receive mentor reviews.
  • Peer-Led Learning — emphasis on collaboration and growth within cohorts.
  • Google Classroom Integration — all resources and assignments are organized for easy access.

Students are expected to self-learn core concepts from notes and reach out during sessions for clarification and extra help.
